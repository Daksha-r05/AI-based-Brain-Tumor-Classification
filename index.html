AI Based Brain Tumor Classification Using CNN

About

This website leverages state-of-the-art deep learning (CNN) to classify brain MRI scans into four categories: glioma, meningioma, pituitary tumor, or no tumor. Upload your MRI scan and receive an instant, AI-powered prediction.

How it works: Our model analyzes the uploaded MRI image and predicts the presence and type of brain tumor, if any. This tool is designed to assist radiologists and patients by providing a fast, second opinion.
